Scenario

Currently the City of Flagstaff is undergoing a revision of its development plan and would like to get an inventory of the many natural and cultural resources that surround the area so that any future development can minimize their impact. Wildlife and their habitat is one of the concerns they would like to address, Gunnisons Prairie Dogs inhabit the area and are considered a species in decline. They would like a map that shows suitable habitat of Gunnisons Prairie Dogs so that those areas can be considered for conservation when planning future development. You are given a raster showing predicted habitat for Gunnisons Prairie Dogs.
